# Owner — Nazarii

> [[Home]] > [[AI-Profiles/README|AI Profiles]] | Related: [[AI-Core-Manifesto]], [[AI-Work-Split]], [[Tasks/In Progress|In Progress]]

## 1. Identity

- **Canonical role:** Human Final Authority
- **Mission:** connect the AI team to real-world systems, business priorities, and launch reality

## 2. Primary Responsibilities

- define business priority
- define desired outcome and urgency
- approve or reject plans
- perform manual external portal steps
- confirm real expected behavior in live conditions
- make final go / no-go decisions

## 3. Ownership Boundaries

### Owns

- GitHub Secrets
- 1Password and real credentials
- Google Cloud Console
- Telnyx portal
- Stripe dashboard
- App Store Connect
- Google OAuth setup
- real billing and production accounts
- real-device verification

### Must Not Delegate

- real secrets to AI
- independent AI control of third-party production accounts
- final launch authority

## 4. Activation Triggers

- whenever a task needs priorities, approval, or a business decision
- whenever real portals, credentials, or devices are required
- whenever final live verification is needed

## 5. Inputs

- concise task summaries from AI
- clear list of external/manual steps
- tradeoffs when multiple valid paths exist
- exact blockers rather than vague technical dumps

## 6. Outputs

- business direction
- priority calls
- manual portal actions
- confirmations from real systems/devices
- final launch or release decisions

## 7. Collaboration Rules

- gives business direction directly and/or through AI 6 product framing
- approves final plans after the review gate
- performs real-world checks after AI 3 QA and before final go/no-go
- should not be forced to infer hidden external blockers from long technical notes

## 8. Non-goals

- does not hand real secrets to AI
- does not replace the planning lane with ad-hoc chat alone
- does not stay the only PM, compliance translator, and growth strategist once AI 6 / AI 7 / AI 8 are in place

## 9. Escalation Rules

Escalate to Owner when:

- real credentials or portal actions are required
- business priority conflicts remain
- there is a launch, billing, legal, or customer-impacting tradeoff
- final device or production-like verification is needed

## 10. Success Criteria

Owner is successful when:

- priorities are clear
- external blockers are resolved explicitly
- final decisions are grounded in real-world verification
- AI lanes stay coordinated instead of chaotic
