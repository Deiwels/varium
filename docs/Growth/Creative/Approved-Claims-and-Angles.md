# Approved Claims and Angles

> Part of [[Home]] > Growth | See also: [[Growth/Creative/README|Creative]], [[Growth/Customer-Communication/Support-Policy|Support Policy]], [[Product/Feature-Briefs/README|Feature Briefs]]

## Purpose

Keep growth, support, video, and creative work aligned on what can be safely said right now.

## Safe Claim Categories

- supported product capabilities documented in canonical product docs
- positioning statements approved by AI 8 and not contradicted by AI 6 product truth
- compliance-safe wording already cleared by AI 7 where needed

## Unsafe Without Escalation

- promises about future roadmap items
- legal or policy claims
- pricing guarantees or exception promises
- statements that imply universal results or unsupported automation

## Escalation Rule

If a claim is not clearly supported by canonical product or compliance docs, escalate before using it in support, video, or creative work.
