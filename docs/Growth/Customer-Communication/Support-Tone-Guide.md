# Support Tone Guide

> Part of [[Home]] > Growth | See also: [[Growth/Customer-Communication/Support-Policy|Support Policy]], [[Growth/FAQ/Customer-FAQ|Customer FAQ]], [[08-Runbooks/Support/Email-Reply-Workflow|Email Reply Workflow]]

## Tone Goals

- calm
- respectful
- clear
- non-defensive
- accurate

## Writing Rules

- answer the user's actual question first
- avoid making the user feel blamed
- use short, scan-friendly paragraphs
- never fake certainty
- say what happens next when escalation is required

## Avoid

- promising timelines without approval
- legal-sounding certainty where policy truth is not confirmed
- vague filler that hides uncertainty
- sharp or robotic language
