# Creative Production Workflow

> Part of [[Home]] > Runbooks | See also: [[Growth/Creative/README|Creative]], [[Growth/Video/README|Video]], [[Growth/Creative/Approved-Claims-and-Angles|Approved Claims and Angles]]

## Purpose

Define the safe flow for turning campaign goals into creative and video assets without bypassing product truth or compliance constraints.

## Step-by-step Procedure

1. AI 8 defines the campaign objective, audience, and CTA.
2. AI 6 is consulted if product framing or scope is ambiguous.
3. AI 7 is consulted if claims, messaging legality, or policy-sensitive wording is involved.
4. AI 11 creates static creative concepts and variants.
5. AI 10 creates video concepts and scripts when motion content is needed.
6. Owner approves sensitive or brand-critical final assets before publishing.

## Guardrails

- creative execution does not redefine strategy
- support, video, and creative agents do not invent product truth
- compliance-sensitive claims route through AI 7
- product-sensitive messaging routes through AI 6

## Expected Outcome

- campaign assets move quickly
- strategy stays centralized
- product truth and compliance boundaries stay intact
