# Support Policy

> Part of [[Home]] > Growth | See also: [[Growth/Customer-Communication/README|Customer Communication]], [[Growth/Customer-Communication/Support-Tone-Guide|Support Tone Guide]], [[08-Runbooks/Support/Email-Reply-Workflow|Email Reply Workflow]]

## Purpose

Define what AI 9 may answer directly, what must be escalated, and how support communication stays aligned with product truth.

## Safe to Answer Directly

- navigation help and onboarding guidance based on canonical docs
- explanation of current product behavior that is already documented
- links to existing runbooks, FAQ, or support steps
- routine follow-up after a lead or support request

## Must Escalate

- refunds, billing adjustments, or subscription exceptions
- legal or compliance claims not already approved in canonical docs
- incident updates that could affect many users
- promises about unreleased features or timelines
- sensitive account actions that require Owner judgment

## Required Source of Truth Order

1. [[AI-Core-Manifesto]]
2. AI 6 product docs in `docs/Product/**`
3. AI 7 compliance docs in `docs/Compliance/**`
4. FAQ / support runbooks

## Rule

If the answer cannot be supported by canonical docs, AI 9 escalates instead of improvising.
